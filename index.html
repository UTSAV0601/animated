About Me

Hi! My name is Utsav Sanghvi. I was born in Gujarat, India and I did my undergraduate in Electronics and Communication from Dharmsinh Desai University. I am a full-time game fanatic, and my love for gaming and technology began in my childhood. The fondness began as I started playing PC games like Road-rash and continued growing with games such as Valorant, Counterstrike, and DOTA. Intrigued by the programming involved in these interactive gaming media, it became my dream to make a game that creates the same indulging real-time experience as it has been for me. I learned and created a 2D Unity Game of my own, the preview of which is present under ‘Projects’. I am excited to take a further step towards my dream career, by pursuing a Master in Computer Science with a specialization in gaming technology and interactive media. During my free time, I also enjoy singing, cooking innovative dishes, and playing outdoor sports like cricket, and badminton. I am currently pursuing my Masters education at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in the field of Interactive Media and Game Development.
